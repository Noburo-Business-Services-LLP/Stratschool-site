About StratSchool

Built from what colleges and founders were missing.

StratSchool was built after years of working with colleges, students, and early founders who needed structured entrepreneurship programs, clearer execution paths, and delivery models that actually led somewhere.

The problem we saw

Workshops existed. Structure did not.

  • Through college programs and campus interactions, we saw that most institutions had activities but not structured founder journeys.
  • Students left workshops inspired, but with no clear path to move from idea to startup.
  • Entrepreneurship support was often fragmented across talks, hackathons, and isolated initiatives.
  • Founders needed practical structure, not more theory-heavy motivation.
What StratSchool built

Programs with structure, pressure, and outcomes.

We built StratSchool to make entrepreneurship learning feel closer to real founder work. That means structured workshops, stronger execution loops, and delivery models for founders, campuses, and public ecosystems.

For founders

Ignite helps people go from idea to startup in 12 weeks through structured pre-incubation.

For ecosystems

Institutional and government programs turn isolated workshops into consistent implementation.
