Curriculum

Validate. Build. Execute. Launch.

Week 1-3

Validate

Customer discovery interviews, problem-solution fit testing, and market sizing.

Deliverable: validated problem statement

Week 4-6

Build

MVP scoping, business model planning, value proposition, and messaging.

Deliverable: MVP plan and build blueprint

Week 7-9

Execute

Outreach systems, first campaigns, user acquisition, and early traction loops.

Deliverable: usable founder execution system

Week 10-12

Launch

Go-to-market, pitch readiness, final showcase, and next-step planning.

Deliverable: startup-ready narrative and launch posture

Ignite is the 12-week pre-incubation track inside StratSchool for founders who want traction, not theory.
