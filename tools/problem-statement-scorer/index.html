Founder validation tool

Score the problem before you scope the product.

Rate the problem on five inputs. The score is weighted to favor urgency and pain slightly more than raw reach.

UrgencyDoes the user need this solved now?
PainHow costly or frustrating is the problem?
ReachCan you identify and reach the audience clearly?
Enter the scores and generate a founder-grade readout.
How to use it
Step 1Score honestly, not optimistically.
Step 2If the score is weak, rework the audience or problem before building.
Step 3Use the interview builder next if the score looks promising.
